WHAT ARE VARICOSE VEINS AND SPIDER VEINS?

Varicose veins are enlarged, twisted and swollen veins often appearing blue or dark purple. Faulty valves in the veins allows blood to flow in the wrong direction and thus causes Varicose Veins.

Spider Veins are small blood vessels that appear in the shape of small lines, clusters or spider web. These appear near the surface of the skin and are in  blue, red or purple color.

First symptoms:

  • Pain
  • Feeling of burning, heat
  • Itching
  • Swelling
  • Night cramps
  • Tingling
